Edible Cookie Dough Recipe

Back to Page 2

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

    • What Next?

    First, heat the flour in the microwave for 1 minute to kill any bacteria. Let it cool.

    In a mixing bowl, mix together the softened butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until smooth. Add the vanilla extract and mix well.

    Gradually add the cooled flour to the butter mixture, stirring until combined. Fold in the chocolate chips.

    Enjoy your delicious edible cookie dough!
